About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research Productivity is an 8‑week live online certification that teaches no‑code AI tools to automate data analysis, content creation, knowledge management and routine tasks.

  • Duration: 8 weeks (next cohort starts May 9, 2025)
  • Workshops: Friday 15:00 GMT or Sunday 17:00 GMT

Course Outcomes

  • Automate document, spreadsheet and data workflows
  • Organize knowledge and accelerate literature reviews
  • Develop custom chatbots and AI‑powered assistants
  • Create professional visuals and presentations with AI
  • Design a hands‑on capstone project to showcase your skills
