About Me

I am a humanitarian and public-health specialist with over a decade of experience leading monitoring, evaluation, research & learning initiatives across Southeast Asia. Currently, I serve as Strategic Information Lead at Community Partners International, empowering community-based organizations along the Thai-Myanmar border through data-driven decision-making.

I hold a Master of Public Health from the National University of Singapore and have managed multidisciplinary teams for UN agencies, INGOs and USAID-funded programs. My professional focus is on leveraging evidence and technology to improve health outcomes for marginalized populations.

Key digital & analytical skills:
